A Local Transport Permit (LTP) is an official document issued by the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) or its implementing offices (CENRO/PENRO), authorizing the transport of wildlife species, wildlife by-products or derivatives, or regulated forest products such as chainsaws and timber within local jurisdictions in the Philippines. It ensures that the transport of such materials complies with Republic Act No. 9147 (Wildlife Resources Conservation and Protection Act) and other relevant environmental regulations. The permit is a safeguard to monitor and regulate the movement of protected or regulated resources to prevent illegal trade, over-extraction, or habitat degradation.
