ðŸ“˜ What is a Certificate of Verification (COV)?

The Certificate of Verification (COV) is issued by the DENR to verify the legal source of planted trees or non-premium species harvested from private lands, which are not covered by a Private Tree Plantation Registration (PTPR). This is also applicable to non-timber forest products except rattan and bamboo.

  • Ensures traceability and accountability of harvested non-timber and timber products
