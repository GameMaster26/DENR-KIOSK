ðŸ“˜ Overview

The Forest Land Use Agreement (FLAg) is a tenurial instrument issued by the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) that grants qualified applicants the right to develop, manage, and utilize portions of forest lands for specific purposes such as agroforestry, ecotourism, and other sustainable land uses. This process ensures the sustainable use and conservation of forest resources in accordance with environmental laws and regulations.

ðŸ“„ Requirements

  • Accomplished FLAg Application Form
  • Project Proposal with map, location, and geotagged photos
  • Proof of land status (e.g., land status certification or inventory of affected trees)
  • Official Receipt of Application Fee (Php 500.00)
  • Endorsement from CENRO and PENRO offices
