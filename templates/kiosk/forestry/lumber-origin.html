ðŸ“˜ What is a Certificate of Lumber Origin (CLO)?

The Certificate of Lumber Origin (CLO) is issued by the DENR for the transport of processed logs/lumber from a Wood Processing Plant (WPP) to another WPP or desired destination. This ensures the legality and traceability of forest products during transport.

ðŸ“„ Purpose of the CLO

  • Verifies the legal source of processed logs or lumber
  • Supports DENR enforcement and monitoring
  • Facilitates inter-WPP transport and lumber trade
