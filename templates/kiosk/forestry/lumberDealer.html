üìò What is a Lumber Dealer Registration Certificate?

A Lumber Dealer Registration Certificate is an official document issued by the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) that authorizes individuals, corporations, partnerships, or associations to legally engage in the sale, trade, or distribution of lumber and other wood-based forest products. This registration is essential for ensuring that all forest products in the market are sourced, handled, and sold in compliance with existing forestry laws and environmental regulations.
