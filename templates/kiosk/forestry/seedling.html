ðŸ“˜ What is a Seedling Request & Technical Assistance

A Seedling Request & Technical Assistance is a service offered by the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) through the National Greening Program (NGP), which allows individuals, communities, or organizations to request seedlings for planting activities. In addition to the provision of planting materials, the service includes technical support such as site assessment, planning, and guidance on proper tree planting and maintenance practices to ensure the success of reforestation and greening initiatives.

ðŸ“„ Purpose of the Seedling Request & Technical Assistance

  • To provide free access to quality seedlings for environmental and community-based greening projects.
  • To assist stakeholders in properly identifying and preparing planting sites for reforestation or enhancement activities.
  • To promote sustainable forest management and increase community involvement in environmental conservation efforts.
