ðŸ“˜ What is a New Wood Processing Plant (WPP) Permit Processing

The New Wood Processing Plant (WPP) Permit Processing refers to the procedure for securing official authorization from the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) to establish and operate a facility engaged in the mechanical processing of wood and forest-based raw materials. This includes activities such as cutting, sawing, slicing, chipping, and other forms of wood conversion.

ðŸ“„ Purpose of the New Wood Processing Plant (WPP) Permit Processing

  • To regulate the establishment and operation of wood processing facilities in accordance with environmental and forestry laws.
  • To ensure sustainable and legal sourcing of raw materials for wood processing operations.
  • To promote the development of the wood industry while protecting forest resources and biodiversity.
