Authentication of Record/s

üìò Overview

Authentication of Record/s is a service provided by the DENR wherein a requesting party (DENR personnel, officials, or external clients) can secure a certified true copy of official records filed in the Records Unit/Section. These authenticated copies are often required for administrative, legal, or official transactions.

Requests must be submitted with the required supporting documents and are processed by the Records Unit/Section in coordination with the appropriate offices. Internal clients (DENR personnel) are exempted from fees, while external clients are required to pay the prescribed authentication fee.
