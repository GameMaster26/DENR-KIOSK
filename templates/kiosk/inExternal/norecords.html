Certificate of No Records/ Appeal /Motion for Reconsideration

ðŸ“˜ Overview

The Issuance of Certification of No Records/Appeal/Motion for Reconsideration is a service provided by the DENR through its Records Unit/Section at the CENRO, Implementing PENRO, PENRO, and Regional Offices.

This certification is issued upon request of DENR personnel, officials, or external parties to confirm that no records, appeal, or motion for reconsideration related to a specific matter are filed with the agency. The request must clearly indicate the purpose for which the certification will be used.

ðŸ“‹ Requirements

  • Duly accomplished customer FOI request form (1 original)
  • Government-issued ID (1 original, for presentation)
  • Additional if from other Government Sector: Official Letter Request (1 original)
  • Additional if Requesting Party is a representative: Special Power of Attorney (SPA), notarized (1 original)
