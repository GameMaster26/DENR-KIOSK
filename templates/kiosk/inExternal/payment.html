Processing of Payment of Claims

üìò Overview

The Processing of Payment of Claims is a financial procedure that ensures the proper preparation, verification, and release of payments for obligations incurred by DENR offices. This includes handling Obligation Requests and Status (ORS), Budget Utilization Requests and Status (BURS), Disbursement Vouchers (DV), and the issuance of Checks/LDDAP-ADA along with corresponding advices.

This process is designed to guarantee that payments to internal personnel, contractors, service providers, suppliers, and other beneficiaries are processed accurately, legally, and in compliance with applicable budgeting, accounting, and auditing regulations.

üìã Requirements

  • Completed Obligation Request and Status (ORS) or Budget Utilization Request and Status (BURS)
  • Approved Disbursement Voucher (DV)
  • Supporting documents for payment, such as official receipts, contracts, or service reports
  • Authorization and approval from the concerned Accounting, Budget, or Finance units
