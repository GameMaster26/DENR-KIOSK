üìò Overview

The Free Patent Application is a government service under the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) designed to provide qualified individuals legal ownership over untitled public agricultural lands they have been occupying and cultivating. This service is governed by laws such as the Public Land Act (CA No. 141), Republic Act No. 782, RA 11231 (Agricultural Free Patent Reform Act), and RA 11573.

Through this process, eligible landowners may obtain a Free Patent Title without undergoing court proceedings, provided they meet the documentary and procedural requirements. The process may be facilitated either at the CENRO or Implementing PENRO depending on the administrative structure in the locality.

üìã Permit Requirements

  • Duly accomplished Free Patent Application form (1 original)
  • Notarized Special Power of Attorney (if filed by a representative)
  • Tax Declaration in the applicant's name or supporting documents such as:
    • Deed of Sale
    • Extra Judicial Settlement
    • Waiver of Rights
    • Deed of Donation
  • Certification of land status from LRA (if applicable)
  • Documentary Stamp (to be attached to the application form)
  • Survey Plan, Technical Description, and A&D Certification from CENRO/PENRO
