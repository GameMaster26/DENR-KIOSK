üìò Service Overview

The Survey Authority is a formal document issued by the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) authorizing licensed geodetic engineers to conduct land surveys for various purposes, including land titling, development planning, or compliance with legal requirements. This authority ensures that surveys are conducted in accordance with national standards, regulations, and approved control points.

Obtaining a Survey Authority is often the first step in land disposition, especially in cases involving government-owned lands or applications for Free Patents. It safeguards the integrity of land boundaries and promotes accurate land records.
