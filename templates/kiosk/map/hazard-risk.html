⚠️ Hazard & Risk Maps

📘 Overview

Hazard and Risk Maps identify areas vulnerable to natural hazards such as landslides, floods, and earthquakes. These maps are essential tools for disaster risk reduction and planning, often coordinated with DENR and other government agencies.

🌋 Common Hazard Types

  • Flood-Prone Areas
  • Landslide Susceptibility
  • Earthquake Fault Zones
  • Typhoon and Storm Surge Paths
