PENRO supports coastal and marine environmental protection by promoting community-based livelihood projects that align with sustainable practices. These include alternative income sources that reduce pressure on marine ecosystems, especially in coastal and protected areas.

While the Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources (BFAR) oversees fisheries management, PENRO collaborates through coastal resource programs, reforestation, and inter-agency support to improve the socio-economic resilience of fisherfolk and coastal residents.
